Observation House Coaching: Transforming Lives Through Sports

The Observation House Coaching initiative by the Sandesh Borde Sports Foundation is a dedicated effort to support children in conflict with the law at the Yerawada Observation House in Pune. Officially appointed by the Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru Udyog Kendra, the foundation began conducting sports coaching and physical training (PT) sessions starting October 2, 2021, to guide these children toward a positive path.

The program focuses on regular sports and PT sessions designed to shift the mindset of children who were previously on a path of crime. By engaging them in physical activities, the foundation has observed a noticeable positive change in their behavior. These sessions promote discipline, physical health, and a sense of purpose, helping the children redirect their energy from negative influences to constructive outlets like sports.

"There is a positive change in the behavior of the children after these sessions. Their minds have shifted from crime to sports and physical health." — Sandesh Borde Sports Foundation

A highlight of this initiative was the organization of a Sports Day at the Yerawada Observation House, featuring special guest Jay Dudhane, a Splitsvilla 13 winner and Marathi Bigg Boss runner-up. His presence as a motivator inspired the youth, reinforcing the power of sports as a transformative tool. Additionally, the foundation arranged a special screening of the movie *Jhund* within the Observation House, in collaboration with the film’s team, including director Nagraj Popatrao Manjule. This event, attended by real-life hero Prof. Vijay Barse, moved the children emotionally and motivated them to promise a commitment to becoming good citizens.

The Observation House Coaching initiative reflects the foundation’s mission to rehabilitate and empower youth through sports and meaningful engagement. By bringing role models like Vijay Barse to interact with the children and offering consistent coaching, we aim to provide them with the tools and inspiration to build a brighter future, breaking free from their past challenges.
