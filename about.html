About Our Foundation

Empowering Communities Through Sports and Education

The Sandesh Borde Sports Foundation is dedicated to promoting education, sports, social reforms, and public health across Maharashtra without any discrimination of caste, creed, or language. Established with a vision to uplift communities, we focus on creating opportunities for youth and the underprivileged through sports and knowledge dissemination, all without monetary gain.

Our efforts include organizing sports tournaments, coaching camps, and social welfare programs to foster talent, health, and unity. From supporting children in conflict with the law to empowering kids in challenging environments, we aim to transform lives by channeling energy into positive outcomes.

Our Goals

Objectives of the Foundation

Sports Promotion

Develop and propagate traditional, national, and international sports across Maharashtra through tournaments, camps, and training institutions.

Education & Health

Advance knowledge in health, fitness, and alternative medicines like yoga and naturopathy, alongside educational initiatives for all.

Social Impact

Provide relief during natural calamities, organize social awareness campaigns, and support the needy with resources and opportunities.
