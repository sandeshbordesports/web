Project Disha: Transforming Lives Through Sports and Guidance

Project Disha, is a transformative initiative by the Sandesh Borde Sports Foundation in collaboration with the Pimpri Chinchwad Police Commissioner Office. This project aims to support children in conflict with the law—formerly referred to as juveniles—by providing counseling, sports coaching, and opportunities to become responsible citizens.

The foundation actively works with four police stations in the Pimpri Chinchwad area, 32 Football teams were formed from 72 slums in Pimpri Chinchwad with the help of 22 police stations in PCMC. Through regular counseling sessions, we help these children identify and nurture their interests, steering them away from negative habits and towards positive life choices. The goal is to equip them with the tools and mindset to reintegrate into society as productive individuals.

"We help them leave bad habits and inculcate good habits. Sports and fitness coaching twice a week have become a powerful medium to channel their energy positively." — Sandesh Borde Sports Foundation

A key component of Project Disha is its sports and fitness program. Since its inception, we have started coaching sessions twice a week, attended by almost 450+ children across the participating police stations. These sessions not only promote physical health but also instill discipline, teamwork, and a sense of purpose. The impact is evident as children shift their focus from past mistakes to building a brighter future through sports.

Project Disha goes beyond traditional rehabilitation by fostering a supportive environment where children can dream big. By collaborating with the Pimpri Chinchwad Police, the foundation ensures that these young individuals receive both emotional guidance and practical skills. This initiative reflects our broader mission to empower communities through sports and education, making a lasting difference one child at a time.
