Project Sanvedana: Empowering Kids in Pune’s Red-Light Area

Project Sanvedana is a heartfelt initiative by the Sandesh Borde Sports Foundation aimed at uplifting the children of sex workers in Pune’s red-light area, specifically Budhwar Peth. The core idea behind this project is to provide these vulnerable kids with positive outlets to prevent them from falling into the cycle of crime and despair that often surrounds their environment.

The foundation recognized that if children from stable societies can veer toward negative paths like crime, murder, or robbery, the risk is even higher for kids growing up in challenging circumstances like Budhwar Peth. To counter this, Project Sanvedana was launched with a mission to transform their negative energy into positive potential through creative and physical activities. One of the first steps was organizing a drawing competition, which the children thoroughly enjoyed, sparking their creativity and engagement.

"If kids from good societies can go on the wrong path, there is a high possibility of kids from such areas getting involved in criminal activity. We aim to change that through sports and creativity." — Sandesh Borde Sports Foundation

Beyond drawing competitions, the foundation plans to introduce sports activities to further engage these children. Sports serve as a powerful tool to build confidence, teamwork, and discipline, offering them a constructive way to channel their energy. The initiative seeks to create a supportive environment where these kids can discover their talents, build self-esteem, and envision a brighter future away from the hardships of their surroundings.

Project Sanvedana embodies the foundation’s broader commitment to social welfare and youth empowerment. By providing opportunities that might otherwise be out of reach, we aim to break the barriers of circumstance and offer these children a chance to thrive. This project is a step toward building a community where every child, regardless of their background, can dream big and pursue a positive path in life.
