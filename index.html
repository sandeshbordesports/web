About Our Foundation

Transforming Lives Through Sports

The Sandesh Borde Sports Foundation is committed to promoting education, sports, and social reforms across Maharashtra. We aim to create opportunities for youth and communities by organizing sports events, coaching camps, and social welfare programs without any discrimination.

From supporting children in conflict with the law to empowering kids in red-light areas, we strive to convert negative energy into positive outcomes through sports and education.
