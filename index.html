Our Mission and Vision

Mr. Sandesh Borde

Founder

Mr. Sandesh Borde, the visionary founder of the Sandesh Borde Sports Foundation, is dedicated to transforming lives through sports, education, and social welfare across Maharashtra. Believing sports can shape character and unite communities, he established the foundation to foster inclusive development without discrimination. His leadership drives events like the Open Tennis Ball Cricket Tournament and Football Tournaments, promoting discipline and personality growth among youth.

Mr. Borde spearheads transformative initiatives like Project Disha, supporting children in conflict with the law, and Project Sanvedana, empowering kids in Pune’s red-light area. Since October 2021, his Observation House Coaching at Yerawada has guided youth toward rehabilitation. From health campaigns to inspiring events like the Jhund screening, Sandesh Borde Sports Foundation’s impact grows under his guidance.
